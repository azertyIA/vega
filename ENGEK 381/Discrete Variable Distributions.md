There are a few discrete variable distributions (expectation, variance):
1. Bernoulli(p): 1 has probability p ($p$, $p(1-p)$)
2. [[Binomial Distribution|Binomial(n, p)]]: total of $n$ Bernoulli hits ($np$, $np(1-p)$)
3. [[Geometric Distribution|Geometric(p)]]: Bernoulli till success ($1/p$, $(1-p)/p^2$)
4. [[Poisson Distribution|Poisson(Î»)]]: $\lambda^xe^{-\lambda}/x!$, the var and exp values are the same: $\lambda$
